Understanding the Table

  • ACS = Average Combat Score. Higher is generally better.
  • Std Dev = Standard Deviation. Lower means more consistent game-to-game performance.
  • CV (%) = Coefficient of Variation. Lower CV means the player is more consistent relative to their average.

Notes:

  • Yui is a flex player (plays multiple agents). Higher Std Dev is natural due to different roles each match.
  • Juicy is a Duelist. Duelists can have bigger form swings (one bad game impacts ACS heavily).
